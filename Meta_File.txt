Project Overview
PrepTrack is a full-stack web application designed to help students and administrators manage exam preparation resources, track academic progress, and share notes, question papers, and results efficiently.
The system includes two distinct portals:
->Student Portal: for accessing resources, checking leaderboards, tracking progress, and viewing results.
->Admin Portal: for uploading materials (notes, PPTs, results), managing students, and publishing announcements.
